🏭 BOM Data Model

Standard Job Architecture — Multi-Level Bill of Materials
SLItems Master
All parts & assemblies (Item, Description, UM, PMTCode)
SLJobs Type='S'
Standard Job — BOM container linking item to routing + materials
SLJobRoutes Operations
OperNum 10, 20, 30... with work centers, setup & run hours
SLJrtResourceGroups + SLJrtSchs Resources
Resource group per operation, Setuprgid + SchedDrv alignment
SLJobmatls Materials
Children linked via OperNum + Sequence. RefType: I/J/P
Multi-Level: Parent material RefType=‘J’ for sub-assemblies. Build bottom-up (leaves first).
Job Format: BOM prefix, 10 chars total. BOM0000001 through BOM9999999.
PMTCode: M (Manufactured), P (Purchased), T (Transferred) only.
ItwhJob: Must set explicitly — links item to standard job in UI.
